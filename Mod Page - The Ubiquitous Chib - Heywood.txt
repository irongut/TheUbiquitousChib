Mod Page: The Ubiquitous Chib - Heywood

Category = Vendors
Name = The Ubiquitous Chib - Heywood
Language = n/a
Version = 1.0.0
Author = Irongut
Overview = Adds a vendor to the alley behind The Time Machine in Heywood who sells all melee weapons and relevant mods.
Description = 
Are you a Heywood salaryman shuffling between the office and your conapt in Wellsprings every day?
Does your boss disrespect you because you don't have a ceremonial sword or Tanto?
Are you a major league mercenary late for a meeting at Embers but you forgot your Neurotoxin knife?
Do you desire the fear and respect on the street that comes with a Chainsword?

Then come on down to TUC Heywood, behind The Time Machine where our expert staff will help you find the melee weapon of your dreams!
Our local contacts deliver new stock all the time so check back regularly! (1 hour refresh)

Also Available:
The Ubiquitous Chib - Watson
The Ubiquitous Chib - Dogtown
The Ubiquitous Chib - North Badlands


Files
Upcoming Merc
Level appropriate melee weapons like a vanilla vendor with Rare and Epic Blade, Melee, Thrown and Blunt mods. Designed for use from the start of the game to respect gameplay balance.

Legend of NC
Legendary (Tier 5) melee weapons and mods. Designed for use at level 60 or if you don't care about gameplay balance.

INSTALL ONE.
Both versions overwrite each other so you can swap between them as you progress through the game if you want to.

Requirements
ArchiveXL
Codeware
RED4ext
redscript
TweakXL
VendorsXL

How to Install
Install all the requirements.
Download EITHER the Upcoming Merc (level appropriate) OR the Legend of NC (all legendary) archive from the files section.
Unpack the zip file into the "Cyberpunk 2077" folder so that the "TheUbiquitousChib_Heywood.yaml" file ends up in the "Cyberpunk 2077\r6\tweaks" folder.

How to Uninstall
Delete the "TheUbiquitousChib_Heywood.yaml" file from the "Cyberpunk 2077\r6\tweaks" folder.
