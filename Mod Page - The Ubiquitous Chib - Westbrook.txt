Mod Page: The Ubiquitous Chib - Westbrook

Category = Vendors
Name = The Ubiquitous Chib - Westbrook
Language = n/a
Version = 1.0.0
Author = Irongut
Overview = Adds a vendor to the corner of Grant & Lele in Westbrook who sells all melee weapons and relevant mods.
Description = 
Are you worried about the homeless camps encroaching on North Oak?
Do the paparazzi break into your mansion but your security won't let you carry a gun?
Would the gift of a ceremonial Kukri or Tanto seal that important business deal?
But you're intimidated by the gang presence outside Spector Cheng's gym?

Then come on down to the observation platform on the corner of Grant & Lele where our expert staff will help you find the melee weapon of your dreams!
Our local contacts deliver new stock all the time so check back regularly! (1 hour refresh)

Also Available:
The Ubiquitous Chib - Watson
The Ubiquitous Chib - Dogtown
The Ubiquitous Chib - North Badlands
The Ubiquitous Chib - Heywood


Files
Upcoming Merc
Level appropriate melee weapons like a vanilla vendor with Rare and Epic Blade, Melee, Thrown and Blunt mods.
Designed for use from the start of the game to respect gameplay balance.

Legend of NC
Legendary (Tier 5) melee weapons and mods. Designed for use at level 50+ or if you don't care about gameplay balance.

INSTALL ONE.
Both versions overwrite each other so you can swap between them as you progress through the game if you want to.

Requirements
ArchiveXL
Codeware
RED4ext
redscript
TweakXL
VendorsXL

How to Install
Install all the requirements.
Download EITHER the Upcoming Merc (level appropriate) OR the Legend of NC (all legendary) archive from the files section.
Unpack the zip file into the "Cyberpunk 2077" folder so that the "TheUbiquitousChib_Westbrook.yaml" file ends up in the "Cyberpunk 2077\r6\tweaks" folder.

How to Uninstall
Delete the "TheUbiquitousChib_Westbrook.yaml" file from the "Cyberpunk 2077\r6\tweaks" folder.
